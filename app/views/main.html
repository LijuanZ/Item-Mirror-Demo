Welcome to Happy Driver

Happy Driver is a tool to manage all of your carpool ride information in one place. Your rides' information will be stored in a folder names "happy-driver" in your Dropbox and categorized by month of year. You can share your rides' informaiton with your passengers by sharing the folder.
